Why Rocket League Is Addictive

By: Cade Lawless (an affected user)

If you have ever played video games you know the feeling of winning to be quite amazing. You also know the feeling of loss to feel like a kick to the axels, speaking in Rocket League terms. If you read my last article (The Best Cars in Rocket League), you know that I am pretty invested into this game. With that being said, I can let you in on a little secret that this game is very addictive.

  • Reasons Why the Game is so Addictive
    1. If you win a match, you get a sense of victory and pride
    2. You then crave that feeling again and again; it makes you play again
    3. If you lose a match, you get a little triggered; you want to keep playing to make up for your losses
  • Ways to Avoid Addiction
    1. There are none
    2. Please reach out if you find a way
    3. It is taking over my life

This game is very well-created and I give all the credit to Psyonix and Epic Games. They both played a great part in making this game so dang addictive. I hope they will continue to do great work within this community and that Rocket League will never die.
