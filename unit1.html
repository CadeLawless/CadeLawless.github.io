Best Cars in Rocket League

Rocket League is a free-to-play video game that involves various different rocket-powered vehicles playing soccer or "soccar." There is a small amount of cars that beat out all others in the game in terms of a few different factors.

These factors include things like turn radiuses, shot power, dribbling, aerialing, hitboxes, and speed. All vehicles in Rocket League have strengths and weaknesses in these categories. The following three cars, in my opinion, are the best in the game, as their strengths heavily outweight their weaknesses.

Best Cars to Use in Rocket League(Ranked in Order)

  1. Octane
  2. Fennec
  3. Dominus
Why Use the Octane?
Octane
  • It is easy to dribble with
  • Its turn radius is one of the best in the game
  • It has a good hitbox that allows you to dribble and aerial with ease
  • Overall, it is the most balanced car in the game
Why Use the Fennec?
Fennec
  • It feels like the fastest car in the game
  • It has great flick power
  • It is a very loose-feeling car which allows for quick turns and decision-making
  • It is a very good mix of the Octane and the Dominus
Why Use the Dominus?
Dominus
  • It has a great deal of power on any shot
  • It is very fast in the air
  • Because of how flat it is, it is easy to pop the ball into the air
  • It can help you make a lot of flashy plays becasue of its hitbox and aerial control
